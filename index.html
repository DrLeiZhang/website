[Our mission] We aim at proposing efficient, effective and intelligent models, algorithms and systems with application to artificial intelligence (machine learning, machine vision, olfaction and taste). Currently, we particularly focus on statistical machine learning models (transfer learning, hashing learning, zero-shot learning, representational learning) and deep learning algorithms (transfer learning/domain generalization/out-of-distribution extrapolation, few-shot learning/meta learning, adversarial learning, convolutional neural networks, unsupervised domain adaptation) for open-world computer vision tasks (non-ideal visual/image classification, trustworthy face recognition/analysis/synthesis, and unrestricted object/pedestrian recognition/detection) and machine olfaction tasks (electronic nose for odor/smell recognition and electronic tongue for taste recognition), and try to make our tiny endeavor for artificial intelligence (the 4th Technological Revolution). We have published more than 100 papers in TOP journals, such as IEEE Transactions (TPAMI, TNNLS, TIP, TMM, TCSVT, TCYB, TSMCA, TIM, TCAS) and TOP conferences (e.g. ICCV, CVPR, ECCV, AAAI, ACM MM, IJCAI, etc). We always welcome any collaborations and highly motivated students (B.Sc, Master, Ph.D, Postdoc) join us in research and development.

[note]: Our group is looking for 6 students per year (for Master or Ph.D degree) with a strong interest in research. There are also 2 PostDoc positions available per year. Research Interests

Machine Intelligence, Computer Vision, Machine Learning/Pattern Recognition, Signal/Image Processing, Machine Olfaction/taste,

Key Research Direction

Machine Learning (human brain):

Deep learning, transfer learning/domain adaptation/domain generalization/Out-of-distribution extrapolation, hashing learning, adversarial learning, cross-view/modal learning, zero-shot learning, subspace learning, discriminative/generative learning, metric learning, extreme learning

Machine Vision (human eye):

Computer vision-object recognition/image classification/face recognition/visual retrieval, object/pedestrian/face detection, person re-identification/search/tracking, image/face synthesis, intelligent video surveillance, classification/recognition/regression, pattern recognition

Machine Olfaction and Taste (human nose and tongue):

Multi-sensor system, information fusion, electronic noses/tongue, sensor arrays, odor recognition, taste recognition
